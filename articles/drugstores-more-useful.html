Japan Services Worth Traveling For

Japanese drugstores are more useful than many tourists expect because they solve practical travel problems before those problems become mood killers.

They are not exciting in the way famous landmarks are. They are valuable in the way a good trip actually needs.

Quick answer

Drugstores become important when the traveler realizes how many minor problems they can absorb. That makes them one of the strongest “quiet value” categories in Japan travel.

Why this feels different

The selection often feels denser, more organized, and more immediately useful than expected. Instead of wandering through vague retail, the traveler can often identify a problem and fix it fast.

Why MeetJapan should highlight it

This is exactly the kind of local-value category that a sightseeing-first site would overlook. MeetJapan gets stronger when it explains the places that improve real travel life.
