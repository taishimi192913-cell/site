# MeetJapan Short To Article Roadmap

## Goal
- Use Shorts to create curiosity
- Move users to practical articles
- Monetize with affiliate links before depending on AdSense

## Phase 1
- Publish 7 high-intent articles
- Add one clear affiliate slot per article
- Keep article layout mobile-first and low-stress

## Phase 2
- Connect social profile links to the site homepage
- Track which article gets the most clicks from each Short
- Rewrite only the pages that prove demand

## Best-first monetization order
1. eSIM / travel connectivity
2. Hotels / stay-area guides
3. Activities / tours / passes
4. Travel essentials
5. AdSense later

## Best-first article topics
- Mobile Suica
- Tax-free shopping
- ATM guide
- Luggage forwarding
- Tattoo-friendly onsen
- Local vs express trains
- Don Quijote shopping guide
